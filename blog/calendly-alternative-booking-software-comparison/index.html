Best Calendly Alternative for Service Businesses (2026): Bookable.live vs Calendly vs SimplyBook.me

If you're looking for a Calendly alternative that's built for service businesses — with SMS reminders, staff scheduling, online payments, and a branded booking page — this comparison will help you decide.

Why look for a Calendly alternative?

Calendly is a solid scheduling tool for meeting-heavy teams. But if you run a service business — a salon, clinic, gym, or freelance practice — you've probably noticed the gaps: no built-in SMS reminders on the free plan, per-seat pricing that adds up fast with staff, limited booking page customization, and no native payment collection.

That's why many service businesses search for a Calendly alternative that gives them more for less. Below, we compare three popular options: Bookable.live, Calendly, and SimplyBook.me.

Bookable.live — the Calendly alternative built for service businesses

Bookable.live is designed specifically for appointment-based businesses: salons, clinics, gyms, therapists, freelancers, and home service providers. Instead of per-seat pricing, you get one flat fee for your entire team.

  • Free plan: Google Calendar sync, email reminders, branded booking page, follow-up messages.
  • Pro plan (€19/mo): SMS reminders (50/mo included, buy more), multiple staff & venues, Stripe payments, custom branding, widget embed, smart rescheduling.
  • No per-seat cost: Adding 5 staff members doesn't multiply your bill — it's still €19/mo.
  • SMS included: 50 SMS/mo in Pro. Need more? Buy credit packs from €5.

Calendly — great for meetings, less so for services

Calendly shines at scheduling internal and external meetings. It's widely adopted in tech, consulting, and sales. But for service businesses with multiple staff, client-facing booking pages, and reminders, it starts to feel limited:

  • Per-seat pricing: $10/seat/mo on Standard, $16/seat/mo on Teams. A salon with 3 staff pays $30–48/mo.
  • SMS reminders: Paid add-on ($10/mo+), only available on higher tiers.
  • No native follow-ups: You need Zapier or third-party automations for post-appointment messages.
  • Booking page: Clean but limited. Not as customizable for service businesses.

SimplyBook.me — flexible but complex

SimplyBook.me has a large feature set, but most features are paid add-ons you enable individually, making it harder to predict costs:

  • Free plan: 50 bookings/mo, limited features. Google Calendar sync requires a paid plan.
  • Paid plans: Start at ~$8.25/mo (billed yearly). Individual features (staff, payments, SMS) are toggle-on add-ons with extra costs.
  • SMS: Paid per credit. No SMS included in any plan.
  • Complexity: The add-on model means dashboard complexity grows fast. Setup takes longer for non-technical users.

Who should use which tool?

  • Choose Bookable.live if you run a service business (salon, clinic, gym, therapy, freelance) and need a simple, affordable booking system with SMS, payments, and multi-staff — without per-seat fees.
  • Choose Calendly if your main use case is scheduling meetings (sales calls, interviews, team syncs) and you don't need service-specific features.
  • Choose SimplyBook.me if you need very specific niche features (e.g., memberships, gift cards, class packs) and don't mind managing add-ons and higher complexity.

Frequently asked questions

Is Bookable.live really free?

Yes. The Regular plan is free forever — it includes Google Calendar sync, email reminders, a booking page, and follow-up messages. Upgrade to Pro (€19/mo) only when you need SMS, payments, or multi-staff.

How many SMS are included?

Pro includes 50 SMS/mo. If you need more, purchase credit packs: +50 for €5, +100 for €8, or +200 for €15.

Do I need to pay per staff member?

No. Unlike Calendly, Bookable.live charges a flat fee. Add unlimited staff on Pro — no extra cost per seat.

Can I accept payments through my booking page?

Yes. Connect Stripe on the Pro plan to accept full payments or deposits when clients book online.
