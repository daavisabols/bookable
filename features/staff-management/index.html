Features / Staff management

Staff management

Manage schedules for teams and venues. Bookable.live helps you organize availability per staff member, so clients can book the right person at the right time.

Unlimited staff members

Add as many team members as you need on the Pro plan. No per-seat fees—one flat rate covers your whole team. Grow without extra cost.

Individual schedules

Set unique working hours and time off for each team member. Part-timers, full-timers, seasonal staff—manage them all from one dashboard.

Service-to-staff mapping

Assign which services each staff member offers. Clients only see available staff for their chosen service—simple and smart.

Client choice (optional)

Let clients choose their preferred staff member, or auto-assign them. You're in control. Some businesses prefer choice, others prefer consistency.

Track team performance

See booking stats per staff member. Who's busiest? Who needs more availability? Data-driven scheduling makes everyone more efficient.

Centralized management

One dashboard for everything: all bookings, all staff, all reminders. No switching between apps. Keep your whole team organized in one place.
