Payments

Enable payments or deposits during booking (where available) to reduce no-shows and make the booking experience smoother.

Accept full or partial payments

Charge full price upfront or collect deposits at booking. You decide the payment terms. Deposits increase commitment and reduce no-shows.

Multiple payment methods

Accept Apple Pay, Google Pay, Visa, Mastercard, and more. Clients get fast checkout—no friction, no delays.

Powered by Stripe

Payments are processed securely through Stripe. Funds go straight to your account. No hidden fees, no payment processor markups.

Instant confirmation

Payment succeeds instantly. Clients see confirmation immediately. No waiting, no uncertainty.

Build trust with clients

Clear pricing and upfront payment show professionalism. Clients know exactly what to expect and what they're paying for.

Track revenue easily

See all payments in your dashboard. Export data anytime. Clear picture of your earnings and booking trends.
